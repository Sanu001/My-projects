Welcome to the Arena

Clash Royale is a real-time strategy game where players collect and upgrade dozens of cards featuring the Clash of Clans troops, spells, and defenses you know and love.

Tower Defense

Knock the enemy King and Princesses from their towers to defeat your opponents and win trophies.

Epic Rewards

Earn chests to unlock rewards, collect powerful new cards and upgrade existing ones.

About Royale Wiki

Royale Wiki is a fan-made project dedicated to providing the most accurate and up-to-date information for players looking to climb the ladder. Our mission is to help every player, from Arena 1 beginners to Ultimate Champions, understand card mechanics and meta-strategies.

Whether you are looking for the hitpoints of a P.E.K.K.A or the travel speed of a Log, we have you covered.
